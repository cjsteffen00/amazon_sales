# amazon_sales
Kaggle dataset data insights.

Possible business questions
- Which product categories generate the highest average discount and do they correlate with higher sales (rating_count)?
- Do higher discounts actually lead to better product ratings?
- Which products have high traffic (rating_count) but low ratings, indicating quality issues?
- Are certain user segments (user_id patterns) consistently giving lower or higher ratings?
- Which products have strong review sentiment (review_title/content) but low conversion (low rating_count)?
